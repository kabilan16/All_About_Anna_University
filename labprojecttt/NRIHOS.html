NRI HOSTELS

Exclusive hostel for students admitted under CIWGC / NRI / FN categories are available. All the rooms are equipped with air conditioner, geyser, a mini-fridge and a microwave oven. Washing machines have been provided in a common room. In addition, the hostel premises continuosly monitored by surveillance cameras. A kitchenette facility has been established to cater to the taste buds of the young engineers residing in the hostels.
