CEG playground

CEG has a huge sports ground in its campus. It is vert spacious and contains a gallery that seats 1000 people and a mini gallery that seats 500 people. It has An Athletic track(400m) and a football pitch with two goalposts.Two standard size basketball courts are also present in the sports ground.



Various sports can be played and the sports equipments are given to CEG students for free and a fully functional air conditioned gym is also present below the main gallery
