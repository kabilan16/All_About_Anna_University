MAIN BUILDING

Famously known as the 'red building', this building is the landmark of CEG and everyone identifies anna university by this building. This is the oldest building in CEG and it was built in 1794. Classrooms for 1st and 2nd semester students are present here and the drawing halls can also be found here. Dean's office is situated in the first floor of this building.
